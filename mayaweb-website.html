About MayaWeb

A Team of Perfectionists in Chandigarh

MayaWeb began with a simple frustration: great photos being let down by rushed, careless editing. So we built a studio that treats every pixel seriously — in Chandigarh's IT Park, serving brands and photographers around the world.

Over five years we've edited more than 40,000 images for 10,000+ clients across 20+ countries. From single hero shots to 10,000-image catalogs, our 15+ editors deliver work that's consistent, natural and on-brand — never over-processed.

We're not the cheapest, and we don't try to be. We're the studio you call when the result actually matters and the deadline is real.
